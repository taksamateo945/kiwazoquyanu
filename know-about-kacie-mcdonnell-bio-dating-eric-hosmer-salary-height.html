Kacie McDonnell is an American journalist, who anchors the sports-related programs on New England Sports Network (NESN). She previously worked as a pre/postgame host for the American soccer club, Sporting Kansas City.

Kacie McDonnell Bio; Family, Education, Height

Kacie was born on 30 July 1990 to parents, Ken McDonnell and Michele McDonnell. She was raised by her family in Pottsville, Pennsylvania along with her siblings; a sister named Isabelle McDonnell and a brother named Lochlan McDonnell.

She pursued her primary education from Nativity BVM High School in 2008. Later, she went to Villanova University and graduated with a BA in Communication at the age of 22. The talented anchor possesses an alluring physique, standing at the height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.60 meters).

Her Career & Salary

While pursuing her University education, Kacie worked as the On-Air Model for QVC, a retail company. In 2012, she joined Fox Television as a traffic reporter and later took the responsibility as a morning anchor.

Kacie served as a reporter for KSHB/KMCI for two years until 2012. She also worked as a courtside reporter at the 2016 NCAA Men’s Final Four basketball tournament. She now anchors various programs at NESN that include NESN Live, NESN Sports Today & NESN Sports Update.
