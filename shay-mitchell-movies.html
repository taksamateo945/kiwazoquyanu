Know About Shay Mitchell; Movies, TV shows, Husband, Age, Daughter

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Shay Mitchell and her partner, Matte Babel, welcome their baby daughter. After suffering a miscarriage, the actress was successful in delivering her first child, and the news was made public through her Instagram post.

Canadian actress and model, Shay Mitchell is known for her role as Emily Fields in the 2010 American teen drama, Pretty Little Liars. The successful show ran for almost seven years, ensuring good fame and fortune towards the artist.

Shay Mitchell Bio & Family

The actress was born as Shannon Ashley Garcia Mitchell on 10 April 1987 in Mississauga, Ontario, to parents, Precious Garcia and Mark Mitchell. Her dad is a Scottish and Irish descent while her mom is Filipino, from the province of Pampanga. Apart from her parents, she has a younger brother named Sean.

Shay went to Rockridge Secondary School and then attended West Vancouver Secondary School to complete her graduation. She stands at the height of 5 feet 7.5 inches (1.71 m) tall and weighs around 60 kg.

Career & Net Worth

Shay has a net worth of $3 million from modeling and acting career since 2007. Before commencing a proper acting career, she went to Bangkok, Hong Kong, and Barcelona, to ally with different modeling agencies. She then chose to return to Toronto to study acting.

The artist has many movie credits like The Possession of Hannah Grace (2018), Dreamland (2016), and Verona (2010), to name a few. Apart from her silver screen work, her TV credits are Pretty Little Liars (2010-2017), Glee (2012), and You (2018).
