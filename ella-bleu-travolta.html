Ella Bleu Travolta Career, Family &amp; Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-03-21

Ella Bleu Travolta, daughter of John Travolta and Kelly Preston, grew up relatively sheltered from the paparazzi.

She made her screen debut in 2001 and continued to have a normal life until 2009 when she starred in her first movie ‘Old Dogs’ alongside her parents and Robin Williams.

The year was also marked by the tragic death of her elder brother Jett, which impacted the family greatly.

After this incident, Ella retreated from the limelight for a few years.

However, she is now making a comeback with the lead role in the thriller ‘The Poison Rose’ (2019), and she was seen on the red carpet for the first time since her brother’s death at the 2018 Cannes Film Festival.

The movie ‘The Poison Rose’ features a cast of A-list actors such as John Travolta, Morgan Freeman, Brendan Fraser, and Famke Janssen. Ella portrays the lead character Rebecca Hunt in the film.

Her co-stars have praised her for being a natural in front of the camera.

Ella Bleu Travolta Shared Same Stage With Her Parents

In 2009, Ella Bleu Travolta portrayed Emily Greer in the ensemble comedy film Old Dogs. John Travolta starred as Charlie Reed and Kelly Preston played Vicki Greer, Emily’s mother.

In real life, Kelly and Ella are mother and daughter. Despite not playing the father, John’s character was best friends with the Greer family.

Ella Bleu Travolta starred alongside her father in multiple movies, including the 2019 thriller film, The Poison Rose.

When she was just eight years old, she had the unique opportunity to act alongside both of her parents.

This made her one of the few actresses who have shared the same acting stage with their mother and father.

Her Net Worth

Ella Travolta, born on April 3, 2000 in the United States, is a well-known movie actress. Her net worth as of January 9, 2023, is $5 million.

In 2001, she starred in the short documentary Not Under My Roof with her mother and Olivia Newton-John.

Summary

Ella Bleu Travolta is a 23-year-old American actress who gained popularity for her role in the 2009 film ‘Old Dogs’.

She was born to Hollywood stars John Travolta and Kelly Preston and had a passion for acting from a young age.

Despite only appearing in two films, she has received extensive media attention due to her red carpet appearances and TV show appearances.
