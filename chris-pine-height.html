Chris Pine Height: How Tall is the Star Trek and Wonder Woman Actor?

American actor Christopher Whitelaw Pine is a gifted performer who has received praise for a variety of theater and cinema performances.

He was born on August 26, 1980, in Los Angeles, California.

Gwynne Gilford and Robert Pine, both successful actors in their respective fields, raised him in a household of artists.

Pine’s grandpa Max M. Gilford was a lawyer and a former president of the Hollywood Bar Association, and his grandmother Anne Gwynne was a well-known Hollywood actress.

Chris Pine, who attended the Oakwood School, earned an English degree from the University of California, Berkeley in 2002.

Pine’s discovery of his love for acting during his time in college led him to start appearing in plays; as a result, he later went on to study at the American Conservatory Theater in San Francisco and attend the Williamstown Theatre Festival.

When he was chosen to play James T. Kirk in the Star Trek reboot film series, he landed his breakthrough role.

He returned to the part in later sequels, garnering praise from critics for his interpretation of the legendary character.

Additionally, Chris Pine became well-known for his parts in other significant motion pictures, including Unstoppable, Wonder Woman, and Hell or High Water.
