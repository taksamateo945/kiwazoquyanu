14-Year-Old Brianna McCarthy Dies Following Local Hit-and-Run Crash

Publish date: 2024-04-04

In a devastating incident that has shaken the local community, 14-year-old Brianna McCarthy has tragically succumbed to injuries sustained in a hit-and-run crash on Saturday night near the intersection of Woodman Drive and Burkhardt Road.

The Incident

On the evening of December 2nd, Brianna was out walking with her friends when she was struck by a car whose driver immediately fled the scene. In the wake of this heart-wrenching event, Brianna was rushed to Miami Valley Hospital in Dayton, Ohio, where she fought for her life in the ICU.

Despite showing slight signs of being able to breathe on her own and some unexpected movement during brain testing, Brianna ultimately could not overcome her injuries and passed away.

A Life Remembered

Brianna, described as a bright, funny, and caring child, was beloved by everyone who knew her. In the days following the accident, the community has been flooded with stories of Brianna’s kindness and the joy she brought to those around her. Her father, Kevin McCarthy, has expressed his profound grief over the loss of his daughter, who he described as “his entire heart.”

Appeal for Help

As the family grapples with the insurmountable medical bills related to Brianna’s ICU stay, missed work, and the expenses related to providing round-the-clock support for Brianna, they are reaching out to the community for help. A fundraising campaign has been launched to help alleviate the financial burden, and any support would be greatly appreciated.

Justice for Brianna

Meanwhile, the Riverside Police are vigorously investigating the hit-and-run incident and are urging anyone with information about the crash or the suspected driver to come forward. As the community mourns the loss of Brianna, they also hope for justice in this tragic case.
