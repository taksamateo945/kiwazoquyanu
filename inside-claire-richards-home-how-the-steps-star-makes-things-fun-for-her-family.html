Inside Claire Richards' home: How the Steps star makes things fun for her family

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Claire Richards shares her not so humble abode with her husband Reece Hill, and two children Charlie and Daisy.

Their quirky home is located in Surrey, and showcases Claire’s vibrant personality.

Just recently, Claire took to Instagram to share with her 154,000 followers images of their house decorated for Christmas, complete with a huge tree decorated with ornamental sweets and candy canes.

In a post on December 7 which amassed almost 9,000 likes, Claire stated: “Thank you Jason and @inspirationswholesaleuk @leafandvineltd for helping me choose the perfect decs for this year’s tree and getting them here so quickly. Placement and decoration by little old me, though. More is more as usual.”
