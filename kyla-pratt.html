Kyla Pratt Biography

Kyla Pratt is best known as a film actress. She became famous with Dr. Dolittle and The Proud Family in her roles. Other awards include Fat Albert, Rehab Road and Hotel for Dogs. She was born on September 16, 1986 in Los Angeles , CA. She appeared in the UPN One on One show from 2001 to 2006. Ray J appeared on the show, too. She started her acting career at the age of eight when she appeared in a series of Nike advertisements. She covered the DisneyMania 2 album single “Circle of Life.”
