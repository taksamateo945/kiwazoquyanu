Rudy Touzet Drops Music Video for New Single Play Me Again (Premiere) Celeb Secrets

Publish date: 2024-05-23

LA-based, Miami born pop singer Rudy Touzet is making a name for himself with each release as he serves infectious single after single that could set any dance floor ablaze. His latest electro-pop track “Play Me Again” serves as no exception and Celeb Secrets is excited to be sharing the first look of the music video!

The visual shows the sleek crooner on and off the stage in his element while exuding confidence and style as he serenades a love interest. The track describes the feeling you get when you’re with someone and you never want that moment to end just like your favorite song. You can play it all you want but you just can’t seem to get enough. If you’re just discovering this pop act, he is certainly one to watch.

“‘Play Me Again’ means a lot of things to me but the song really resonates with that feeling of wanting to have the person you like, continuously thinking about you and wanting to be with you,” Touzet tells Celeb Secrets exclusively.

“Behind the music video idea, we really just wanted to put on my “dream” performance that I was thinking in my head while a record was playing. It’s me prepping before the opening night of my show.”

Rudy Touzet kicked off the new year strong with the release of his sultry dance track “Fatal Attraction.” Inspired by a real-life experience of spending the night with a woman twice his age, the track details his attraction to someone who knows their worth, what they need, and won’t stop until they get it.

Over the past year, Rudy has released an array of music with producers Luke Shrestha and Simon Jay which have amassed millions of streams In the coming weeks, Touzet will release his new single “Contigo” featuring Teddi and we have no doubt Rudy Touzet is only just getting started turning up the heat.

“I’m really excited for people to listen to ‘Contigo!’ It’s a fun song that will get you dancing and the lyrics are very catchy. I want fans to dance around wherever they are when Contigo comes on!” he shares.
