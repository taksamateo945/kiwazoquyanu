Take That,RAYE,&amp; S Club all confirmed for Capital's Jingle Bell Ball with Barclaycard!

Publish date: 2024-04-26

The Sunday night line-up for Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball with Barclaycard has been announced, and it is EPIC!

Performing at the O2 Arena on December 10th will be Take That, Rita Ora, RAYE, Joel Corry, S Club, Rudimental, Zara Larsson, and Jazzy. 

Already confirmed to play night one on Saturday, December 9th are Alicia Keys, Tom Grennan, Busted, Tate McRae, Becky Hill, ArrDee, Leigh-Anne, Nathan Dawe, and Jax Jones.

Commenting on this years event, Founder and Executive President of Global Ashley Tabor-King OBE says: “It’s great to be back with Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball, the UK’s biggest Christmas party!” 

“This year is going to be huge as we bring together some of the biggest artists in the world, some of whom have never appeared at a Capital Ball before, to perform at this very special event.” 

“With acts, including Alicia Keys, Take That and Leigh-Anne, Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball with Barclaycard is the official start of Christmas!”

James Rea, Chief Broadcasting & Content Officer at Global, adds: “Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball is one of the most anticipated music events of the year and we’re thrilled to be celebrating Christmas with such a great line-up.” 

“It’s going to be another incredible weekend and we can’t wait for our listeners to start the festive season, partying alongside their favourite artists.”? 

Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball with Barclaycard will take place at The O2 in London on Saturday 9th, and Sunday 10th December, with performances from the world’s biggest music stars. 

The show will also be streamed live for fans on Global Player.

Tickets will go on sale on Friday 17th November at 8am on Global Player. Download Global Player on your smartphone or tablet from the App Store or Google Play.
