Akira Toriyama Married Life With His Wife Nachi Mikami

Nachi Mikami and Akira Toriyama married in Japan on May 2, 1982. They had been married for 42 years before Akira died on March 1, 2024, at the age of 68. According to kanzenshuu, a Dragon Ball documentation site, he had been dating her for a year and was “shy” around women, but felt he could be “himself” with her.

They further allege that the married couple authorized local media to photograph the “small” wedding but only if they did so in “five minutes”. When asked about his wife at the time, he claimed she has “a good personality” and “looks after his well-being”.

Yoshimi Kato and Akira Toriyama had two children together. They have two children: a son, Sasuke, born on March 23, 1987, and a daughter, born in October 1990.
