EXCLUSIVE: Premiere of JayWalker's New Single 'Kickin' Back On A Sunday'

Publish date: 2024-05-25

JayWalker are a fast-rising duo consisting of the heartbeat from the award-winning band Hey Romeo; Darren Gusnowsky and Rob Shapiro, together they create a modern-day sonic sound that also incorporates the best of 90s Country. They effortlessly blur a number of genres to deliver heartfelt and authentic songs.

The duo are the fresh new face of Country and they combine their natural chemistry formed over almost two decades of writing, recording and performing together to create a unique sound that showcases their outstanding individual and combined abilities.

They burst onto the burgeoning scene with their brilliant single ‘Kickin’ Back On A Sunday’ which officially drops this Friday, August 23rd and here at CelebMix we are thrilled to bring you the world exclusive premiere of the track ahead of its official release.

Speaking about the single, JayWalker say: “The inspiration for the song came from only having one day off a week on the road. That is usually Sunday. On the way home the guys always ask what I am going to do with the day off, my reply would always be,” Just kickin’ back and hanging with my wife and kids” Can’t think of a better way to spend the day”.
