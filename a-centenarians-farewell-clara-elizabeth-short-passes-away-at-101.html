A Centenarians Farewell: Clara Elizabeth Short Passes Away at 101

Publish date: 2024-06-09

In a sorrowful announcement, the family of Clara Elizabeth Short has shared the news of her passing. Clara, fondly known as “Grandma Betty,” peacefully passed away at the age of 101 in Indianapolis, Indiana on December 16, 2023.

Clara was born on December 16, 1922, Clara lived an incredible and remarkable life. Over her century-long journey, she touched the hearts of many with her kindness, resilience, and love for life.

Her loved ones remember Clara as a woman of strength and compassion. Her legacy will continue to live on through the memories shared by her family, friends, and all those whose lives were touched by her.

News of Clara’s passing has left the community in mourning. As they bid farewell to this remarkable woman, they also celebrate the extraordinary life she led, her contributions to the community, and the lasting impact she made on those around her.

In memory of Clara, a memorial fund has been set up to honor her life and legacy. The fund aims to support causes close to Clara’s heart, further testament to the indelible mark she left on her community and loved ones.

Our deepest condolences go out to Clara’s family and friends during this difficult time.
