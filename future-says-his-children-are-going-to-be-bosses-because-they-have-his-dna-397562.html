Future Says His Children Are Going To Be Bosses Because They Have His DNA

Publish date: 2024-02-18

Fans of the Atlanta, Georgia artist, Future, know that he’s been making waves ever since his rap career first began. However, in recent years, the rapper has become even more famous for other reasons related to his personal life.

Particularly, his many children and baby mommas. Future recently dropped a brand new record, High Off Life, and amid the album’s release, Future talked at length about his family life and what he has in store for his kids.

Regarding his album, Future’s new effort features the likes of some of the biggest names in the game, including Youngboy Never Broke Again, Lil’ Uzi Vert, Young Thug, DaBaby, and Drake, among others. Just a few minutes after it was released, Future’s album reached Gold status Hot New Hip Hop reported.

While promoting his record, Future stopped by the set of Zane Lowe’s Beats 1 Radio in which he addressed his new album in addition to his aforementioned family life which has admittedly caused controversy in the rapper’s life.

Fans of Future know that he doesn’t mention his many children that often, however, he thinks they’re all going to be “bosses” in their own right because they’ve been imprinted with his own DNA. The rapper believes they’ll likely appreciate music and have talent for it, because he loves being in the studio.

It’s what he does. There’s nothing he loves in the world more than making music with people he’s close too. Furthermore, Future touched on his family life which he jokingly described as the Jackson 5. Regarding the future of his family, the rapper stated, “and their DNA, there’s boss in their DNA. So it’s a problem.”

In case you missed it, Future was in the headlines earlier this week once again when it was revealed that he had another child, Reign Wilburn, with Eliza Reign.
